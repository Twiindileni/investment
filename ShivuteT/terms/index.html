Daily Earnings: Earnings are calculated based on the amount invested. For every N$500 invested, clients will earn N$50 per day. For every N$1,000 invested, clients will earn N$100 per day. For every N$5,000 invested, clients will earn N$500 per day. For every $10,000 invested, clients will earn $1,000 per day.

Withdrawal Process: All withdrawal requests will be processed within 1 hour of submission. Clients must have a minimum balance of N$500 to request a withdrawal.

Fees: Horizon Capital charges a management fee of 1% of the total investment amount per month. This fee is deducted from the client's earnings.

Investment Duration: There is no fixed investment duration. Clients can choose to withdraw their funds at any time.

Account Security: Horizon Capital uses the latest encryption technologies to secure client accounts and transactions. Clients are responsible for maintaining the security of their account login credentials.

Communication: Horizon Capital may communicate important information regarding client accounts and investments via email or through the client portal.
